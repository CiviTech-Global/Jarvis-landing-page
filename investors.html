Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from potential investors.

What stage is Jarvis at?

Jarvis has completed its core product development with 17 fully functional features, a polished Ember Dark design system, and cross-platform support via Flutter. We are in the pre-launch phase, preparing for public release alongside our Series A raise.

How will Jarvis be monetized?

We plan a freemium model with a free tier covering core wellness and productivity tools, and a premium subscription unlocking advanced AI insights, wearable integrations, detailed analytics, and priority support. We're targeting a 5–10% premium conversion rate, with an enterprise B2B tier planned post-Series A.

What is the go-to-market strategy?

Our multi-channel strategy includes organic app store optimization, influencer partnerships with wellness creators, strategic integrations with major health platforms (Apple Health, Google Fit, Fitbit), and regional marketing campaigns targeting North America, the Middle East, and Asia. We'll also leverage content marketing and community building to drive organic growth.

What are the main risks?

Key risks include competition from established players (Apple Health, Google Fit, Samsung Health), evolving data privacy regulations, user retention challenges inherent to wellness apps, and execution risk in new markets. Our privacy-first architecture, superior design, and AI personalization are our primary mitigation strategies.

How can I perform due diligence?

We provide a comprehensive data room with product metrics, technical architecture documentation, cap table, financial projections, and regulatory compliance details. Schedule a call with our team to request access and discuss the opportunity in detail.
