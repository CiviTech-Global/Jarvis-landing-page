Meet Your AI Assistant

Jarvis is an all-in-one AI-powered companion designed to help you take control of your daily wellness and productivity. From managing your tasks and tracking your habits to monitoring your health metrics and guiding your meditation practice.

Built with a beautiful Ember Dark theme, Jarvis brings together 17 carefully crafted tools into one seamless experience. Every feature is designed to be intuitive, insightful, and empowering.

Whether you're focused on fitness goals, building better habits, or simply staying organized, Jarvis adapts to your lifestyle and helps you make smarter decisions every day.

Every Feature, Up Close

Explore the 15 tools that make Jarvis your ultimate daily companion.

Tasks & To-Do

Manage your daily tasks with priorities, due dates, and a visual progress ring that keeps you motivated throughout the day.

  • ✓ Priority-based task organization
  • ✓ Visual progress tracking ring
  • ✓ Smart filters — Today, Upcoming, Completed

Focus Timer

Stay in the zone with a Pomodoro-style timer. Choose Focus, Short Break, or Long Break modes with customizable durations.

  • ✓ Focus / Short / Long break modes
  • ✓ 15, 25, and 30-minute sessions
  • ✓ Visual countdown with progress arc

Habit Tracker

Build lasting habits across Health, Fitness, and Mindfulness categories. Track streaks and stay consistent.

  • ✓ Categorized habits with color coding
  • ✓ Daily streak tracking
  • ✓ Weekly consistency view

Weather

Get real-time weather data with humidity, wind speed, UV index, and hourly forecasts at a glance.

  • ✓ Real-time temperature and conditions
  • ✓ Humidity, wind, and UV index
  • ✓ Hourly forecast timeline

Water Intake

Stay hydrated with an animated wave progress tracker. Quick-add water amounts and get daily hydration tips.

  • ✓ Animated wave progress visualization
  • ✓ Quick-add buttons (150ml, 250ml, 500ml)
  • ✓ Daily hydration tips and reminders

Calorie Tracker

Monitor your daily calorie intake with a 2000 kcal goal ring. Quick-add foods and track macros effortlessly.

  • ✓ Visual calorie goal ring
  • ✓ Quick-add foods with calorie counts
  • ✓ Macro tracking — protein, carbs, fat

Sleep Monitor

Track your sleep patterns with a beautiful moon visualization. Quick-log bedtimes and get personalized sleep tips.

  • ✓ Moon-themed sleep visualization
  • ✓ Quick-log bedtime and wake time
  • ✓ AI-powered sleep quality tips

Step Counter

Hit your 10,000-step goal with a visual progress ring. Track distance, calories burned, and active minutes.

  • ✓ 10K daily goal progress ring
  • ✓ Distance and calorie tracking
  • ✓ Active minutes monitoring

Workout Timer

Multi-sport workout timer supporting Running, Cycling, Strength, Yoga, Swimming, and Walking modes.

  • ✓ 6 sport modes with custom icons
  • ✓ Duration and calorie tracking
  • ✓ Session history and streaks

Heart Rate Monitor

Track your BPM with zone detection. Quick-log heart rate readings and monitor your cardiovascular health.

  • ✓ BPM monitoring with zone detection
  • ✓ Quick-log heart rate buttons
  • ✓ Resting, normal, and active zones

Mood Tracker

Log your daily mood with a 5-level emoji picker. Track mood streaks and get positivity tips.

  • ✓ 5-level emoji mood picker
  • ✓ Mood streak tracking
  • ✓ Daily positivity tips and insights

Screen Time

Monitor your device usage vs. daily limits. See app breakdown charts and take control of your digital habits.

  • ✓ Usage vs. limit tracking
  • ✓ App category breakdown chart
  • ✓ Digital wellness tips

Meditation

Find your calm with guided breathing exercises, a soothing breathing orb animation, and mindfulness tips.

  • ✓ Animated breathing orb guide
  • ✓ Multiple guided session lengths
  • ✓ Daily mindfulness tips

Dashboard

  • ✓ Personalized AI greeting
